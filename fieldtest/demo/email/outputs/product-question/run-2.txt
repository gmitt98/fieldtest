Yes, Clearbook supports importing from QuickBooks! Here's how it works:

1. Export from QuickBooks: Go to File > Export and select your date range
2. Import in Clearbook: Use Settings > Data Import > QuickBooks
3. Review and confirm: Map your accounts and verify the transaction count

You can bring in your full chart of accounts and transaction history. Most migrations complete in under an hour.
