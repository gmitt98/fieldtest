According to the Meridian Corp Employee Handbook (Section 4.2), new employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per calendar year. These days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month starting from your first day of employment.

A few important things to keep in mind: vacation days do not carry over to the following year unless you have written approval from HR, and you'll need to request vacation at least 5 business days in advance for any period of 3 or more consecutive days.
